# Caption And Hashtag Patterns

Track packaging patterns only after repeated evidence.

## Confirmed Patterns

- None yet.

## Assumptions To Test

- Keep captions short enough to support the video, not compete with it.
- Match the energy of the footage: aggressive, premium, fast, and specific.
- Use a CTA only when it feels native to the post.
- Avoid generic filler such as "thoughts?" unless it consistently performs.
- Prefer a small focused set over a large generic dump.
- Mix broad niche tags with specific machine or format tags.
- Avoid irrelevant trending tags.
- Treat hashtags as discoverability support, not the main growth lever.

## Patterns To Watch

- Short flex captions
- Challenge or comparison captions
- Sound-focused captions
- Luxury or rarity captions
- Broad plus niche hashtag mixes

## Promotion Guardrail

- Any caption or hashtag idea with only one apparent win
- Patterns where the hook or footage clearly carried the post on their own
